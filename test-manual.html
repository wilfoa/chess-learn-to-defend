Manual Test Instructions

Open index.html in your browser to test the Chess Threat Visualizer app.

Features to Test:

  1. ✅ Hebrew text is displayed correctly throughout the app
  2. ✅ Chess board loads with all 32 pieces in starting position
  3. ✅ Pieces can be dragged and dropped to make legal moves
  4. ✅ Illegal moves are prevented (pieces snap back)
  5. ✅ Turn indicator updates after each move (תור הלבן / תור השחור)
  6. ✅ "משחק חדש" button resets the board
  7. ✅ "בטל מהלך" button undoes the last move
  8. ✅ "הפוך לוח" button flips the board orientation
  9. ✅ Threat visualization shows red borders on threatened pieces
  10. ✅ Yellow borders show attacking pieces
  11. ✅ Toggle "הצג איומים" turns threat display on/off
  12. ✅ Radio buttons switch between showing current player threats vs all threats
  13. ✅ Check warning appears when king is in check
  14. ✅ Game over message appears on checkmate
  15. ✅ Captured pieces are displayed

Test Scenario:

Try the Scholar's Mate sequence:

  1. White: e2 to e4
  2. Black: e7 to e5
  3. White: Bishop f1 to c4
  4. Black: Knight b8 to c6
  5. White: Queen d1 to h5
